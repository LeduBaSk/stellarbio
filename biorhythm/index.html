The Science of Biorhythms

Biorhythms are biological cycles that affect our daily lives in three primary areas: physical, emotional, and intellectual. These cycles begin at birth and oscillate in consistent wave-like patterns throughout our lives.

01. Physical Cycle

Physical Cycle (23 days): Governs your energy, strength, and coordination. When high, you feel vigorous; when low, you may need more rest.

02. Emotional Cycle

Emotional Cycle (28 days): Influences your mood, creativity, and sensitivity. This cycle affects how you interact with others and perceive the world.

03. Intellectual Cycle

Intellectual Cycle (33 days): Affects your concentration, memory, and cognitive skills. Ideal for studying, strategic planning, and complex decisions.

Understanding Critical Days

A critical day occurs when a cycle curve crosses the 0% zero line, transitioning from a positive peak to a negative phase, or vice versa. During these crossover days, your energy is unstable, necessitating heightened awareness and focus.
